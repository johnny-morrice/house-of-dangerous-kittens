House of Dangerous Kittens

A survival horror game

Written for Ludum Dare 20

Requirements:
SDL
SDL_mixer
SDL_image
Glib
gcc
rake

Play the game:

rake play

The game is placed in work/kittens

Controls:

Move the player using the directional keys or WASD
Click to shoot
Press p to pause
Escape to quit
